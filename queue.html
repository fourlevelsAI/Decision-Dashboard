Operating rule

This is a weekly ritual. Don’t “clean it up.” Close loops and extract learning.

If reviews keep slipping, you’re training yourself to rationalize instead of learn. A review closes only with: Outcome + Learning.

Priority order
Overdue → Due Soon → Risk Open → Ignored.
What “risk open” means
Impact = High, or Confidence < 60, or High impact + Guardrails missing, or the decision is already overdue.
Where to start
People decisions compound fastest. If you’re unsure, review those first.
